Privacy Policy

TimeDraft is a Chrome extension designed to help users generate availability suggestions from their Google Calendar.

Information Accessed

  • Google Calendar data (read-only) to determine availability.

How Information Is Used

  • Calendar data is used only to generate availability times.
  • No data is modified, stored, or transmitted to external servers.

Data Sharing

TimeDraft does not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

Data Storage

All processing occurs locally within the user's browser. No personal data is stored outside the extension.
